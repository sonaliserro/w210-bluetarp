Our Data

To conduct our analysis, we combined several public datasets, including OpenFEMA’s Individual Assistance Housing Registrants – Large Disasters data, U.S. Census Bureau’s American Community Survey – 5 Year Estimates data, and National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration’s Hurricane data.


OpenFEMA’s Individual Assistance Housing Registrants – Large Disasters

This dataset includes anonymized records of applications for FEMA’s Individual Assistance program during the 2017 hurricane season. The data was derived from the National Emergency Information System (NEMIS), which has been used to process all cases for FEMA’s Individual Assistance programs since 1998. In the dataset, we worked with:

$2.5 billion

paid out in housing assistance

4.6 million

anonymized registrations across the 3 hurricanes
